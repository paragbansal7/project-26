INGREDIENTS for BOMBAY GRILLED SANDWICH for 1 serving:

1 slices brown bread
1/2 handful mint leaves
1 pinches salt
1/2 small tomato
1/2 medium potato
1/4 teaspoon chaat masala
2 tablespoon water
1/2 handful coriander leaves
1 green chilli
1/2 medium cucumber
1/2 small onion
1 tablespoon butter
1 wedges cheddar cheese
